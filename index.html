Drawing On The Grid

drawing

Clicking on on a cells will increment it's color by one, eventually overflowing back to clear. Clicking and holding while dragging the mouse draws the first cell's color on every cell that the mouse drags into.

erasing

Erase by holding shift while clicking (or while dragging) the mouse.

selecting a preset

The presets automatically cycle until you either click on a specific preset or start drawing on the grid. Click on any of the presets at any time to reset the tattoo to that preset.


Frequently Asked Questions

how do i remove my tattoo

To remove your tattoo, just leave the Git-Tattooers organization by going to your organization settings. Note that you cannot rejoin after you've left.

i don't see my tattoo

Be sure to accept the email invitation to the Git-Tattooers organization, check any spam folders. It may take up to 24 hours for a tattoo to appear after accepting the invitation.

can i make my tattoo repo private

Unfortunately the only way for the tattoo to exist is to have a publicly visible repo that is owned by an organization that you are a public member of.

how does this work

A repository is constructed under the Git-Tattooers organization. Commits are then made in a pattern representing the tattoo with the supplied email as the author. For these commits to show up on your contribution calendar, you must be a member of the organization that owns the repository, so you have to accept the invitation to the Git-Tattooers organization.
