Frequently asked questions

Git Tattoo

How do I remove my Tattoo?

On GitHub, leave the "Git-Tattooers" organization. To rejoin the organization, you need to get a fresh Tattoo.

I don't see my Tattoo.

Be sure to accept the email invitation to the "Git-Tattooers" organization. When you made the payment, we sent an inviation to your supplied email address. It may take up to a day for the Tattoo to appear, due to GitHub's slower refresh rate.
